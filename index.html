Sri Lanka's Largest Freshwater Haven

Discover the Tranquility of Bolgoda Lake

Nestled within the lush landscapes of the Western Province, Bolgoda Lake is a pristine paradise that invites you to experience nature at its finest. Spanning over the scenic regions of Colombo and Kalutara, this serene lake is more than just a body of water; it's a vibrant ecosystem teeming with exotic wildlife, rich mangroves, and crystal-clear waters.

Explore, relax, and reconnect with nature at Bolgoda Lake.
